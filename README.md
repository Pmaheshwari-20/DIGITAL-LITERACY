## Project Overview
This repository contains the Digital Literacy project completed as part of the course requirements. The project focuses on understanding digital literacy through practical tasks involving online tools, digital platforms, professional communication, safe internet practices, and cybercrime awareness.

## Module Summaries

### Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
Created a one-page visual resource explaining what digital literacy means and why it matters. The infographic includes useful digital tools for students, safe internet practices, professional online presence, and email etiquette.

### Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio
Created a basic digital portfolio using GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle to build a professional online identity and prepare for future academic and career opportunities.

### Task 3 – Coding Practice and Google Workspace Collaboration
Used HackerRank to complete a beginner-level coding challenge and created a Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz using Google Forms and Google Sheets.

### Task 4 – Professional Emails and Social Media Etiquette
Prepared two professional emails and created a social media do's and don'ts checklist to understand responsible digital communication.

### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness Case Study and Prevention Guide
Created a case study on UPI/Online Payment Fraud and prepared a prevention checklist to promote safe online behavior.
